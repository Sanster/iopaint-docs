**IOPaint** is a free, open-source and fully self-hostable inpainting/outpainting tool powered by state-of-the-art AI models.
IOPaint has the following features:
- Remove any unwanted object, defect, watermarks, people from your pictures
- Make modifications to specific parts of the image: add new objects or replace anything on your pictures.
- Expand your images: generate new pixels to make your images bigger.
